About Us

²markit was born out of the recognition of massive change coming to our local community. Growth and infrastructure investments have attracted large corporations the small local businesses that we grew up with are under threat. Our mission is to keep local business in the community by making modern marketing and web design techniques accessible to small business.

Our team of professionals are all local to the South Delta area. We grew up here. We understand the value that small businesses bring to small communities. We don't want the success of the community to force out the businesses that have supported us for so long. We have a vested interest and responsibility to use our skills to help shape our community.

Our founders baked small local business into the DNA of our company. It's important to us to keep small local business at the heart of what we are so that our clients can trust they are our top priority. Our consultants have first-hand experince with the pressures and challenges of small local businesses. Our method of hands on analysis gets our team into client operations to develop individualized and realistic recommendations for the business.

As a small business ourselves, ²markit is more agile than large consulting firms. This means we're able develop and implement marketing strategies when and where it really matters to our clients. We know that our clients don't have massive marketing budgets so our small teams aim to implement strategies that are cost effective and rapidly deployable.

Our team of local professionals aim to provide industry expertise at price points accessible to small local businesses.
